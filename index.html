About us

Team Evolution is the FIRST Technical Challenge (FTC) team in Hadley, MA. Our members are middle and high school students who work together to build and program robots that accomplish specific tasks.This year, the FTC challenge is called "FIRST Res-Q".

What's happening recently

11/7: Evolution has conquered the Mountain! At a league idea-sharing meet, our tread-based driving system has proved its effectiveness in climbing the ramps on the playing field this year.
